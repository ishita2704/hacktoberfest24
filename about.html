About Hacktoberfest24

Hacktoberfest24 is an open-source initiative aimed at encouraging developers of all skill levels to contribute to open-source projects. Our goal is to foster collaboration, innovation, and community growth through meaningful contributions to open-source software.

Our Mission

Promote Open-Source

We believe in the power of open-source software to bring people together and create impactful projects that benefit everyone.

Encourage Collaboration

Hacktoberfest24 encourages developers from all over the world to collaborate, share knowledge, and work on innovative solutions.

Learn and Grow

Our goal is to provide an environment where developers can learn from each other, enhance their coding skills, and contribute to real-world projects.
